ChatGPT Key Information
======================

Developer: OpenAI
Release Date: November 30, 2022
Platform: Cloud computing platforms
Description: A generative artificial intelligence chatbot

Key Highlights:
- Based on the GPT-4 large language model
- Generates human-like conversational responses
- Fastest-growing consumer software application (100M users in 2 months)
- Current OpenAI valuation: 6 billion
- Sparked development of competing products (Gemini, Claude, Llama, etc.)
